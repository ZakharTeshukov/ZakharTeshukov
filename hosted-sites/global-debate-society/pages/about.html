Our Mission

Empowering voices, fostering critical thinking, and building a global community of debaters.

Who We Are

Global Debate Society is a transnational organization dedicated to promoting debate education and critical thinking skills across Southeast Asia. Founded by passionate debaters and educators, we aim to create a platform where students can develop their argumentation, public speaking, and analytical abilities.
