Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule my tutoring sessions?

After signing up, you'll have access to our scheduling system where you can book sessions with your preferred tutors at times that work for you. You can also reschedule sessions up to 24 hours in advance.

Can I switch tutors if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely! We want you to have the best experience possible. If you're not connecting with your current tutor, you can request a change at any time through your student dashboard.

Is there a minimum commitment period?

For monthly plans, there is no minimum commitment. You can cancel anytime. For semester plans, you're committing to the full semester (typically 4-5 months) to get the discounted rate.

Do unused hours roll over to the next month?

Yes! Unused hours roll over for up to 3 months, ensuring you get full value from your tutoring package even during less busy periods.
